## SECTION 5 — How It Works

**Section number:** 04
**Section heading:** How a project actually *runs*.

Four steps. Each is a row with a big mono number, an H4 title, and a 2–3 sentence description.

**Step 01 — Book the email audit**
Free 20-minute Calendly call. We review your current setup, list health, last 30 days of sends, and offer landscape. You walk off the call with two or three immediate, no-strings recommendations whether we work together or not.

**Step 02 — Voice capture and onboarding**
If we're a fit, the engagement starts with a 60-minute voice capture call. I build the Voice Profile doc, lock the campaign calendar, and get ESP / tooling access.

**Step 03 — First 30 days**
I ship the first batch of emails on a tight feedback loop. You review, I refine, emails go live. By day 30 the voice is locked, the rhythm is set, and the calendar is months ahead.

**Step 04 — Ongoing**
3–5 emails per week. Monthly strategy review. Quarterly campaign planning. You focus on coaching. I focus on email.

## Copy QA pass before launch

Read each section out loud. For every paragraph, ask:

- Does it sound like Rudy talking, or like a chatbot summarising Rudy?
- Is there one verb-led CTA, or am I asking the visitor to do two things?
- Did I sneak in a banned word (see `01-brand-identity.md` §7.2)?
- Is anything italicised besides the one accent word per headline / pull-quote?

If any answer is wrong, rewrite that paragraph before shipping.
